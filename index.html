About Our College

SDMCET, located in Dharwad, Karnataka, India, is a prestigious autonomous engineering college affiliated with the SDM Educational Society. It offers undergraduate courses and is renowned for its high ranking among engineering colleges in India. The campus, spread over 70 acres in North Karnataka, provides a picturesque environment conducive to learning. SDMCET focuses on holistic student development, with departments like Physical Education & Sports Science contributing to nurturing talents. The college places a strong emphasis on leadership, acknowledging the dedication of its faculty and staff in upholding its esteemed position in engineering education.
