Admission Process

Welcome to our college! We are excited that you are interested in joining our community.

To apply for admission, please follow these steps:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents (transcripts, test scores, etc.).
  3. Attend an interview (if required).
  4. Receive admission decision.
  5. Complete enrollment process.

Application Deadlines

Our college has rolling admissions. However, it is recommended to submit your application by the following deadlines:

  • Fall Semester: August 1
  • Spring Semester: December 1
  • Summer Semester: May 1
